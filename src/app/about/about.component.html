What is GreenBat?

GreenBat is a simple tool you can use to look up information related to various CCGs (collectible card games) without worrying about ads doing silly stuff with your browser.

Also, outside of the simple logo for this application in the heading, the only images you'll see are those related to content you're trying to access, so things should run pretty smoothly even with poor connection speeds.
